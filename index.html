About Me

I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon Web Services. I received my Ph.D. degree at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2019, supervised by Professor James T. Kwok. I obtained my M.Phil. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2015. Before joining HKUST, I received my Bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2013.
